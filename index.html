About Me

Bio

Hi! I'm currently a member of the Junior class at Newburyport High School. I created Schumacher Sounds in 2022 as a way to combine mine love of music performance and production. I've has played trumpet and guitar in various groups and ensembles for years, and feel equally at home onstage or backstage.

I've played trumpet since 5th Grade, and I've had the opportunity to perform with the MMEA Senior District Jazz Band twice! I also love playing in my school's pit band for the musical.

I love hiking, especially in the winter, and I'm almost done climbing the 48 High Peaks of New Hampshire!

I taught myself guitar over the last few years, and love playing with my band, Hyperdrive!
